My Experience | | . . .

Team Leader - CODEFEST : Inter College Hackathon March-2023

Birla Institue of Technology, Mesra | Lalpur Extension

  • CODEFEST was a 36 hours inter-college hackathon coding competition organised during the 2 day fest AURORA-23 in BIT Mesra, Lalpur Campus by Information Technology Society. Participants were given to choose from three problem statements and develop a solution for it.
  • Our team chose problem statement 2 that was 'Develop a solution so that students gets motivated for and can carry out their studies effectively'
  • Our group had five members and I was the team leader, we developed a prototype website of a platform that would connect the students ,professors and alumni of the college where professors and alumni can create communities of their respective fields and where students can join and post their queries related to that field and others can answer it. The website also included a study planner and an alumni portal which allowed the students to directly connect to the alumnis for job and internship purposes
  • My Roles :
    (i) Team Leader - Suggested and pitched the idea and presented the website in front of judges
    (ii) Front - End - Some parts of the front-end like the login and registration screen was designed by me using HTML, CSS, JavaScript
    (iii) Back - End - 85% of the backend logic for the website was written by me, where I designed the database using MySQL and for the logic to connect the front-end to the database was written in PHP. We used XAMPP Server Package for developing the website on our local computers.
  • Result: We were awarded as the Winners of problem statement 2
  • Note: As the competition lasted for only 36 hours,therefore, the whole website wasn't completed and it contained only the important functions that were pitched and hence it wasn't deployed
